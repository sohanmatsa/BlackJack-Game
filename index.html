How to play

  • Player will have to HIT or STAY depending on the sum of cards.
  • By clicking HIT you can add more cards one by one . You should not exceed the sum 21.
  • By clicking STAY you will stop adding cards and the Dealer will start their game .If the sum of cards of Player or Dealer is greater than 21, they lose automatically.
  • To win, you need to ensure the combined value of your cards is greater than that of the dealer.
